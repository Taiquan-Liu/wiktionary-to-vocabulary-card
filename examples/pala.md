```ad-note
title: Definition
collapse: collapse
1. piece, bit, part, lump, chunk
pala palalta ― piece by piece, piecemeal
2. (often in compounds) snack (something designed to be eaten quickly, in one go)
3. (idiomatic) of something that is difficult to accept or endure; especially with vaikea or kova
Veronkorotus on äänestäjille vaikea pala.The tax increase is a hard pill to swallow for the voters.
Hänen kuolemansa oli minulle kova pala.Her death was a hard blow for me.
```
