Finnish

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

The inessive singular of Proto-Finnic *taltë-.

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /ˈtɑlːesːɑ/, [ˈt̪ɑ̝lːe̞s̠ːɑ̝]
  • Rhymes: -ɑlːesːɑ
  • Hyphenation(key): tal‧les‧sa

Adverb

[edit]

tallessa

  1. (of state) safe, secure (in a safe place, free from the danger of theft)
    Koruni ovat tallessa lukitussa laatikossa.
    My jewellery is safe in a locked drawer.

Inflection

[edit]
→○ illative talteen
○ inessive tallessa
○→ elative tallesta
