Finnish

[edit]

Etymology 1

[edit]

yskä (“cough”) +‎ -iä

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): /ˈyskiæˣ/, [ˈys̠k̟iæ(ʔ)]
  • Rhymes: -yskiæ
  • Hyphenation(key): ys‧kiä

Verb

[edit]

yskiä

  1. to cough (push air from the lungs in a quick, noisy explosion)
  2. to expectorate (cough up fluid from the lungs)
  3. to cough (make a noise like a cough)
